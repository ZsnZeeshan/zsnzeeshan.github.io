## Data Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with the site and to improve its content and usability.

Google Analytics processes usage data such as page views, device information, and IP addresses.

IP addresses are anonymized before storage. Analytics data is collected and processed **only after you have given your explicit consent**.

Analytics data may be transferred to and processed on servers located outside the European Union. Google relies on Standard Contractual Clauses to ensure an adequate level of data protection.
